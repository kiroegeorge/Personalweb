Portfolio

Project 1

    NutriPoa Company Website
I designed and developed a website for NutriPoa, a company focused on sustainable farming, organic nutrition, and environmental conservation. The website provides information about their projects, including organic recycling, nutrition and diet education, and no-till gardening. It features an intuitive layout, a blog for updates, a team page, and a structured navigation system to enhance user experience. The design ensures accessibility, responsiveness, and ease of content management, making it a valuable tool for NutriPoa's mission of promoting healthier and eco-friendly lifestyles.
